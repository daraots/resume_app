AUK Soccer Tournaments


For four consecutive years, I was the lead organizer of the American University of Kurdistan Intramural and Intercollegiate Soccer Tournaments. From scheduling, to booking the stadium and even procuring an outside sponsor to fund the costs of the tournament. I even recorded and published tournament data on campus to encourage spectators from the student body. See below for some pictures. Notice how there are male and female students in the crowd watching the games. This was inspiring to see, given how the region is conservative and female students don't usually go out to sporting events. We encouraged the launch of a female tournament but we didn't have enough students who wanted to play.
